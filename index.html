Introduction

My name is Montana Mellett, a media designer currently studying at Algonquin College school of Media and Design. Prior to this, I received my Bachelor of Arts in International Development Studies from Trent University. This experience also included a year abroad in Ecuador doing community development at the Universidad de Andina Simon Bolivar.

My range of studies and life experience helps me bring a different perspective to my design. I excel in graphics, user experience, and responsive web design. I tell stories with my camera and inspire connection with my words. Let me tell your story with captivating visuals, a clean brand, and a responsive website!
